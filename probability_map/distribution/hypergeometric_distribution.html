Probability Mass Function (PMF)
$$P(X=k) = \frac{\binom{K}{k}\binom{N-K}{n-k}}{\binom{N}{n}}$$
Properties
Mean$n \cdot \frac{K}{N}$
Variance$n\frac{K}{N}\frac{N-K}{N}\frac{N-n}{N-1}$
