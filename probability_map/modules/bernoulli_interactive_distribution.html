Bernoulli Distribution $X \sim \text{Bernoulli}(p)$
(success prob)
Probability Mass Function
$$P(X=k) = p^k (1-p)^{1-k}, \quad k \in \{0, 1\}$$
Key Properties
Support$k \in \{0, 1\}$
Mean$E[X] = p$
Variance$\text{Var}[X] = p(1-p)$
Skewness$\frac{1-2p}{\sqrt{p(1-p)}}$

PMF $P(X=k)$
