Binomial Distribution $X \sim \text{Bin}(n, p)$
(trials)
(success prob)
Probability Mass Function
$$P(X=k) = \binom{n}{k} p^k (1-p)^{n-k}$$
Key Properties
Support$k \in \{0, 1, \ldots, n\}$
Mean$E[X] = np$
Variance$\text{Var}[X] = np(1-p)$
Mode$\lfloor (n+1)p \rfloor$
