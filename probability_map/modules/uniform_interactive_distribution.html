Probability Density Function
$$f(x) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{b-a} & x \in [a, b] \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$
Key Properties
Support$x \in [a, b]$
Mean$E[X] = \frac{a+b}{2}$
Variance$\text{Var}[X] = \frac{(b-a)^2}{12}$
CDF$F(x) = \frac{x-a}{b-a}$
