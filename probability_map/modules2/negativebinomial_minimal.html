Negative Binomial
(number of successes / shape)
(success probability per trial)
Key facts
  • Support: k = 0,1,2,… (failures before r-th success)
  • PMF: P(X=k) = C(k+r−1, k) (1−p)ᵏ pʳ
  • Mean: E[X] = r(1−p)/p
  • Variance: Var[X] = r(1−p)/p²
  • Sum of r independent geometric (failures) r.v.s
Parameters
r = 5, p = 0.50
E[X] = 5.000, Var[X] = 10.000
