Poisson Distribution
(rate / expected count)
Key facts
  • Support: k = 0, 1, 2, …
  • PMF: P(X=k) = λᵏ e⁻λ / k!
  • Mean: E[X] = λ
  • Variance: Var[X] = λ
  • Limit of Binomial(n,p) as n→∞, p→0, np=λ
  • Sum of Poissons is Poisson: X₁+X₂ ~ Poisson(λ₁+λ₂)
