The Standard Uniform distribution is Uniform(0, 1) ‚Äî the special case where a = 0 and b = 1.

Transformation: Any Uniform(a, b) can be standardized to Uniform(0, 1) by U = (X - a)/(b - a).

Importance: Standard Uniform is fundamental for random number generation and the inverse transform method.
